Retailer Onboarding & Verification

Structured activation keeps distribution accountable from day one

Every retailer is vetted before they touch your products. Our onboarding process combines documentation, interviews, and visual confirmation to protect every brand we represent.

  • Submission of business license, resale certificate, and tax documentation.
  • Storefront photo validation to confirm signage, merchandising, and physical presence.
  • Compliance interview and reference checks to review sales channels and presentation standards.
  • Review and acceptance of the Retail Stride MAP & Channel Policy to ensure pricing integrity.
  • Execution of the Retailer Agreement Contract, followed by assignment of a unique authorization ID for traceable distribution.
