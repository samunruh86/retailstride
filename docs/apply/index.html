We partner exclusively with established, independently owned retailers that operate verified storefronts—prioritizing brand safety and authentic distribution.

To qualify for approval:

Maintain an active, publicly accessible brick and mortar location.

Agree not to sell on third-party marketplaces such as Amazon, Walmart, or eBay.

Provide a valid resale or tax certificate and accept our Authorized Retailer Terms.

Every application is manually reviewed to ensure alignment with brand standards. Only qualified retailers receive wholesale access.
