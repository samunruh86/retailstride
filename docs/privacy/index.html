Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2025

Retail Stride connects verified brands and retailers through curated wholesale access, catalog management, and partner support. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard information when you interact with our website, platform, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the Services:

  • Account and contact information, such as your name, business name, role, email address, phone number, and login credentials.
  • Business profile details that you choose to share about your store, assortment, buying preferences, or brand catalog.
  • Transaction and communications data related to requests for access, catalog inquiries, orders, and messages exchanged with our team.
  • Usage information collected automatically, including device type, browser, IP address, pages viewed, referring URLs, and interaction data that helps us understand how the Services are used.
  • Cookies and similar technologies that store preferences and support analytics or authentication.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Services, including onboarding, catalog access, and partner communications.
  • Verify retailer and brand applications to maintain a trusted wholesale network.
  • Respond to inquiries, deliver updates, and send transactional or service-related messages.
  • Personalize experiences by tailoring recommendations, assortments, or marketing communications.
  • Monitor performance, analyze trends, and maintain the security and integrity of the Services.
  • Comply with applicable laws, enforce our agreements, and protect the rights of Retail Stride, our partners, and our users.

3. How We Share Information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who support our operations (for example, hosting, analytics, communications, and payment processors) under contractual obligations to protect the data.
  • With verified brands or retailers as needed to facilitate wholesale relationships, order fulfillment, or support requests that you initiate.
  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • When necessary to detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues.
  • In connection with a business transaction such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to appropriate confidentiality safeguards.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to authenticate sessions, remember preferences, understand how the Services perform, and deliver relevant content. You can adjust your browser settings to limit or disable cookies, though doing so may affect certain functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information against accidental or unlawful access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Despite these efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to deliver the Services, fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer required, we will delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention practices.

7. Your Choices

You may update your account information, adjust marketing preferences, or request deletion of your account by contacting us. You can also opt out of non-essential marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages.

8. International Users

Retail Stride operates primarily within the United States. If you access the Services from other regions, you understand that your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Services are intended for business use and are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child without appropriate consent, we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. Significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please visit our contact page to get in touch.
