## Retailer Onboarding & Verification  

Every retailer in our network undergoes a structured onboarding process prior to activation.

**Verification Includes:**  
- Submission of business license and resale certificate  
- Storefront photo and signage confirmation  
- Compliance interview and reference check  
- Review and acceptance of MAP & Channel Policy  
- Execution of Retailer Agreement Contract  

Once approved, each retailer is issued a **unique authorization ID** tied to their account.  
This ID enables traceable, controlled distribution‚Äîensuring products never flow into unauthorized channels.
