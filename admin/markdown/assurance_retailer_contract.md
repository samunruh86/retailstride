### **1. Agreement Overview**
This **Retailer Agreement** (“Agreement”) is entered into between **Retail Stride LLC** (“Retail Stride”) and the approved retailer (“Retailer”) for participation in the Retail Stride verified network.  
By signing this Agreement, the Retailer agrees to uphold Retail Stride’s compliance, brand protection, and operational standards.

---

### **2. Purpose**
Retail Stride operates as a **brand-authorized distributor** serving verified brick-and-mortar retailers.  
Our mission is to provide small and mid-sized independent stores with controlled access to national brands while protecting brand integrity and channel alignment.

---

### **3. Eligibility Requirements**
To qualify for participation, Retailers must:
- Maintain an active **brick-and-mortar storefront** with a verifiable address and business license  
- Provide a valid **resale certificate** and applicable tax identification  
- Operate under a consistent business name across all documents  
- Maintain compliance with Retail Stride’s **MAP & Channel Policy**  

Retailers selling exclusively online are **not eligible** without written authorization.

---

### **4. Authorized Sales Channels**
**Retailer may sell only through:**
- Their **own physical storefront(s)**  
- Their **official business website**, if pre-approved in writing  

**Retailer may not:**
- List or sell products on third-party marketplaces (Amazon, Walmart, eBay, etc.)  
- Transfer or resell goods to any unauthorized third party  
- Represent themselves as an authorized distributor or wholesaler  

Retail Stride reserves the right to audit listings and sales activity to ensure compliance.

---

### **5. MAP & Brand Compliance**
Retailer agrees to:
- Adhere to all **Minimum Advertised Price (MAP)** policies set by Retail Stride and its brand partners  
- Maintain brand presentation consistent with approved marketing assets  
- Promptly remove or correct any listings that deviate from compliance standards  

Violations of MAP or brand presentation requirements may result in suspension or termination from the Retail Stride network.

---

### **6. Ordering & Fulfillment**
- All orders are subject to Retail Stride’s acceptance and available inventory  
- Payment terms, freight policies, and return conditions follow Retail Stride’s standard operating terms  
- Retailer is responsible for proper handling and in-store presentation of received goods  

Retail Stride may update order minimums, freight thresholds, or fulfillment processes with reasonable notice.

---

### **7. Termination**
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with written notice.  
Retail Stride reserves the right to immediately revoke participation in the event of:
- MAP or channel violations  
- Misrepresentation of business operations  
- Non-payment or chargebacks  
- Actions that harm Retail Stride or its brand partners  

Upon termination, Retailer must discontinue all use of Retail Stride materials, logos, and brand assets.
